Street craps from the safety of home

Rolls & Rules

0

Roll 7 or 11 on the first throw & win. Roll 2, 3, or 12 on the first throw --"craps"--the player loses. Roll anything else on first throw, that is your "point". To win, you must continue rolling the dice until you "make your point". You lose if you roll a 7 before making the point.
